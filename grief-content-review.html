Voice Guidelines for Grief Responses

Cat oracle - but GENTLE here. Cats sit with you when you're sad. They don't fix it, they're just present.

  • Short, tender
  • NO platitudes ("they're in a better place")
  • NO toxic positivity
  • YES to simple presence
  • YES to acknowledging pain without fixing it
  • Cat behavior: sitting close, not leaving, quiet company

Example tone: "I can't fix it. But I can be here." / "Grief is love with nowhere to go. Let it sit with you."

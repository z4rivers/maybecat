## What Top Rankers Do (Ranking Tactics)
1. **Exact-match domains** — Single most powerful factor in this niche
2. **Below-the-fold SEO text** — History, FAQ, "all answers" lists, invisible to users
3. **Multiple schema types** — WebApplication + FAQPage + AggregateRating + HowTo
4. **Programmatic question pages** — themagic8ball.com generates individual pages for every common question
5. **Multi-language support** — 15-20+ hreflang tags for international traffic

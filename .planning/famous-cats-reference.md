## Comics & Cartoons
- **Garfield** — lazy, sarcastic, food-obsessed, hates Mondays, no shame
- **Sylvester** — perpetual loser, never quits, "sufferin' succotash"
- **Tom** (Tom & Jerry) — elaborate plans, always outsmarted, slapstick genius
- **Top Cat** — smooth-talking con artist, leader, charm over substance
- **Felix the Cat** — resourceful, magic bag of tricks, upbeat
- **Heathcliff** — street brawler, takes what he wants, blue collar
- **Hobbes** (Calvin & Hobbes) — philosophical best friend, wise but playful, imaginary-or-real
- **Catbert** (Dilbert) — evil HR director, delights in bureaucratic cruelty
- **Bill the Cat** (Bloom County) — deliberately gross, "Ack!", anti-Garfield punk rock cat
- **Stimpy** (Ren & Stimpy) — sweet, dumb, happy, pushed the button
- **Pink Panther** — suave, cool, never speaks, everything works out
- **Krazy Kat** — absurdist, loved Ignatz who threw bricks, purest delusional devotion
- **Fritz the Cat** — R. Crumb counterculture, first X-rated cartoon, provocative
- **Azrael** (Smurfs) — scheming villain's sidekick
- **Figaro** (Pinocchio) — cute, mischievous, jealous
- **Pusheen** — round, cute, food, simple pleasures, stickers
- **Simon's Cat** — demanding, physical comedy, pestering the human constantly
- **Scratchy** (Simpsons) — perpetual victim in Itchy & Scratchy ultraviolence
- **Snowball II** (Simpsons) — actually Snowball V, nobody corrects it

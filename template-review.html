Template Review

The Problem: Templates combine patterns + slots to generate responses. The frames often add nothing ("Ancient cat proverb:") and the combinations can be clunky. But some slot content is good on its own.

Options: KEEP template as-is | KILL entirely | EXTRACT good slot content as standalone responses
